About Me

Get in touch

I don't solve problems, I create solutions.


I believe that the future is digital and that living in the era of big data, AI and the unprecedented connectivity of IoT means that only the companies that know how to harness the potential of such computing paradigms to continuously innovate and transform are the ones destined to shine in the coming decade.

However, when it comes to innovation, technology on its own is hardly enough.

With my broad technical background and refined skillset, I bring a human-centric mindfulness that enables teams and companies to innovate and thrive in the ever-changing world of technology. As a firm believer that interdisciplinarity and multi-modal, holistic approaches can bring forth new ground-breaking solutions, I help teams recognise patterns, anticipate needs and trends, focus on the “what if” and the free-exchange of ideas that create an environment which encourages a growth mindset and a diverse collaborative culture of success.
